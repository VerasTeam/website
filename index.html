Veras

The "Fake News" Radar

Veras is a website, mobile application, and browser extension where users work together to hold news organizations accountable. Cite sources and vote on other user's citations to earn rewards.

Power of Community

Work together to stop misinformation

Users work together to determine the factual accuracies and biases of each article submitted on the Veras platform. Users can also post links to other content such as videos for the community to fact check.

Let's stop the spread of misinformation.

Veras is a social media platform which allows for users to work together to rate truthfulness of articles.

Rating Rewards

Pointing out biases and factual inaccuracies will reward the user with a certain amount of coins.

Reward Tiers

Presenting academic credentials and partipating often will give your ratings more influence and reward you with more coins.

Browser Extension

Veras works as a convenient browser extension that allows you view alleged inaccurcies and biases, rate supposed articles, and submit sources for claims.

Distrust in the media has skyrocketed in recent history. We spoke to hundreds of people, many of whom agreed that they couldn't trust the media.

"I don't trust news sources because most are owned by rich men with their hand in multiple different industries and have vested interest in the public knowing (not knowing) certain things."

Daneya J. Student

"I am distressed that some new sources seem to not verify the accuracy of what they report as having acquired from other sources. I lend more weight to certain news sources but I do not 'trust' them."

Anonymous Data Analyst
